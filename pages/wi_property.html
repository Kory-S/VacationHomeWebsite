Beautiful cabin on Lost Lake in St. Germain, Wisconsin. Full kitchen, Wi-Fi, gas grill, and more. Two queen size beds and two twin beds.

Enjoy a quiet getaway in the beautiful northern Wisconsin woods. This cabin offers plenty of recreational activites such as swimming, hiking, tennis courts, and much more. There's is a lot this cabin has to offer for families of all ages. Take advantage of the trails for hiking, biking or ATVs or a quiet evening at the lake or pool. There's also board games, a DVD player, and Wi-Fi for a quiet day in while you take advantage of the gas grill on the porch or full kitchen for meals.
